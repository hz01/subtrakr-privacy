SubTrakr – Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

Last updated: March 20, 2026

SubTrakr ("we", "our", or "us") is developed and maintained by Flexkit. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the SubTrakr mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from your Google account.
  • App data: Subscription information you enter in the app (name, price, billing date, etc.) is stored locally on your device and optionally synced to our secure cloud database (Firebase Firestore) if you choose to sign in.
  • Usage data: We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous usage data such as app opens, screen views, and feature interactions. This data does not identify you personally.
  • Advertising data: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the SubTrakr app experience
  • To sync your subscription data across devices (only if signed in)
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve app performance
  • To display relevant advertisements

3. Data Storage and Security

Your subscription data is stored locally on your device by default. If you choose to sign in with Google, your data is synced to Firebase Firestore, which is hosted on Google's secure infrastructure. All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.

4. Third-Party Services

SubTrakr uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Sign-In: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Firebase (Firestore): firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google AdMob: support.google.com/admob
  • Google Analytics for Firebase: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account or request data deletion, your data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of personalized advertising

7. Account and Data Deletion

To request deletion of your account and all associated data, please email us at support@flexkit.net with the subject line "Delete my SubTrakr account". We will process your request within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

SubTrakr is intended for users aged 16 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
